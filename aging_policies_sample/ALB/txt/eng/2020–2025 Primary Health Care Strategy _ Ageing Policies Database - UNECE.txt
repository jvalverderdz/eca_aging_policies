2020–2025 Primary Health Care Strategy
Strategjia Zhvillimit të Kujdesi Paresor (2020-2025)
Albania 2020
The strategy aimed to improve the quality, accessibility, and efficiency of primary healthcare
services in Albania, and to promote the health and well-being of the population, especially the
most vulnerable groups. It sought to address the challenges and opportunities of ageing, such
as health, social care, social protection, social inclusion, participation, and empowerment. The
strategy was developed by the Ministry of Health and Social Protection. It covered all
individuals and families in need of primary health care services in Albania, with a focus on older
persons aged 60 and over. It was a national and cross-sectoral strategy that involved different
areas and levels of government.
The strategy defined the vision, mission, values, principles, objectives, and actions of primary
health care in Albania, based on the family medicine model. It had six strategic objectives and
24 specific actions, with indicators, targets, responsibilities, and budgets. The actions covered
areas such as health promotion, disease prevention, integrated care, quality improvement,
human resources, financing, governance, and information systems. The strategy was
implemented by the Ministry of Health and Social Protection, with a steering committee, a
technical secretariat, and a monitoring and evaluation system. The timeline was from 2020 to
2025.
